About Memories2Bytes

We are a family-owned business dedicated to preserving your memories. At Memories2Bytes, our passion for technology and nostalgia drove us to create a company focused on transferring analog media to digital formats.

Founded by myself, Jamie Wilson, and my son Shane Wilson, we’ve always loved tech and tinkering. Together, we transformed this passion into a way to help others preserve their cherished memories for future generations.

Why Choose Us?

At Memories2Bytes, we are passionate about preserving your precious memories. Our experienced team ensures that every piece of media is treated with care, using the best techniques and equipment to deliver high-quality digital conversions.

Expert Handling

We treat every tape, cassette, or image as if it were our own, ensuring careful cleaning and expert care in every conversion.

Cutting-Edge Technology

With years of technical experience, we use top-tier equipment to deliver the best quality digital conversion of your media.

A Labor of Love

This isn’t just a business to us—it’s a personal mission to help you save precious moments for generations to come.

This is why we care: We understand that your memories are invaluable, and our dedicated team is committed to preserving them with the utmost care. Utilizing state-of-the-art technology, we ensure the highest quality for every media format. Whether it’s an old video tape, a cherished photo album, or a priceless audio recording, we treat each project as if it were our own. Choosing us means choosing experience, precision, and a deep respect for the memories that matter most to you.


From VHS tapes and audio cassettes to old photos, we convert all forms of media into high-quality digital formats. Whether it’s restoring old home videos or making your favorite mixtapes accessible again, our process ensures the best possible results for every project.

Our goal is simple: to make sure your memories last a lifetime. Whether you're preserving family events or archiving historical moments, we’re here to help make that happen.
