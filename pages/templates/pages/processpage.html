Our Process

Why We Use This Process

Analog media, such as tapes and photos, have a limited shelf life. Over time, these precious memories degrade and can become unplayable. That’s why we follow a detailed process to preserve and restore them before it’s too late. Our proven methods ensure every step is done with precision, ensuring the best possible outcome for your irreplaceable memories.

The Cleaning Process

Before we start converting, every piece of media goes through a thorough cleaning process. We meticulously remove dirt, dust, and even mold from tapes and discs, ensuring that the playback quality is as clear as possible. This step is essential to guarantee that your media is free of any obstructions that could interfere with the digital conversion process.

The Converting Process

Using state-of-the-art equipment, we convert your media into high-quality digital formats. We pay close attention to detail, enhancing video clarity, removing noise, and ensuring the audio is crisp. We know how important these memories are, and that’s why we take extra care to avoid errors during the conversion process, providing you with the best possible result.

Receiving Your Link & Old Media

Once the conversion is complete, we’ll send you a secure download link so you can access your digital files immediately. Alongside this, we carefully package and return your original media, so you can keep both the old and new versions. No need to wait—your memories are ready to enjoy again without any delay.
