Pricing

How We Determine Pricing

Pricing that reflects the unique needs of each project. Our pricing is based on the condition of the media, the type of media being converted, and the quantity of items you want to preserve.

Media Condition

The state of your media can affect the conversion process. We assess each item to determine the care needed for optimal results.

Type of Media

Whether it's video tapes, audio recordings, or photographs, each type has specific requirements and techniques for the best conversion.

Quantity of Media

The total number of items you wish to convert also plays a role in pricing. We offer competitive rates for larger quantities, ensuring affordability for all your preservation needs.

At Memories2Bytes, we believe in transparency when it comes to pricing. On average, the cost for preserving your cherished media is $1 per image, $30 per video, and $25 per audio tape. These prices apply to media that is in good condition, requiring only standard cleaning. For any items that need deep cleaning or additional care, we’ll provide a customized quote to ensure you receive the best value for your investment. Trust us to handle your memories with the attention they deserve.


Media Condition

At Memories2Bytes, we take media condition seriously. When your tapes and other formats arrive, we assess their condition to ensure the best quality conversions.

For media that appears clean, we perform a quick wipe to remove any dust and fuzz. However, if there is mold or flaking on the tapes, we take the time to disassemble them for thorough cleaning. This meticulous process not only prevents contaminants from affecting your final product but also protects our machines from damage. Unlike big box stores that may simply label unplayable media with a red tag, we prioritize quality by ensuring everything is clean before conversion.

Clean Media
Quick wipe for best results.

Moldy Media
Thorough disassembly and cleaning.

Flaking Media
Careful handling to prevent damage.

Audio Conversion

Video Conversion

We convert VHS, Betamax, and more to clear digital video formats for easy sharing.

Image Conversion

We digitize photos, slides, and negatives to preserve your memories.
